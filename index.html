About Siksha Niketan School

Siksha Niketan School (SHIKSHA NIKETANBARADIH PUL) was established in 1996 and is managed by Shivshankar Singh. The school is located in a rural area in the SASARAM block of ROHTAS district, Bihar. We provide education for Grades 1 to 8 and are co-educational. The school does not have an attached pre-primary section. English and Hindi are the mediums of instruction.
